How to Play!

Welcome to the DIY Connections game! Follow these simple steps to get started:

How To

Find groups of four items that share something in common.

Select four items and tap "submit" to check if your guess is correct. Find the groups before making 4 mistakes!

Examples:

TREES: evergreen, oak, fir, maple

FIRE ____: Ant, Drill, Ball, Truck

Category Colors:

The categories are separated by their level of difficulty.

They go in order of yellow, green, blue, and purple from easiest to hardest.
